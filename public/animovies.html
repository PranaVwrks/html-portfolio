BEST MOVIES I WATCHED

These are the top 3 anime movies I watched

Your Name

As expected of any Makoto Shinkai movie, the director of colourful and mind-blowing movies like Suzume, Weathering with You, The Garden Of Words, etc ,your name offers a beautiful romance experience between two highschool teenagers. Their love connected through space and time and red-thread of love with the captivating animations and storyline of the movie are sure to keep you wondering about it for whole night.

Spirited Away

The fanciful adventures of a ten-year-old girl named Chihiro, who discovers a secret world when she and her family get lost and venture through a hillside tunnel. When her parents undergo a mysterious transformation, Chihiro must fend for herself as she encounters strange spirits, assorted creatures and a grumpy sorceress who seeks to prevent her from returning to the human world.
